About Us

The Liberia Students Association in Morocco is a non-profit organization established to promote unity, academic excellence, and cultural exchange among Liberian students studying in Morocco.

Our mission is to provide support, guidance, and a sense of community for Liberian students navigating academic life in Morocco.

We organize various activities including academic workshops, cultural events, and networking opportunities to enhance the student experience.

Legal Assistance - Professional Support

Here are some resources and professional support available to assist with your legal needs:

  • Visa renewal assistance from certified professionals.
  • Guidance on residency permits and immigration services.

Frequently Asked Questions

Any Liberian student currently enrolled in a Moroccan university or higher education institution can become a member. Alumni and prospective students may also join as associate members.
